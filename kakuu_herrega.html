Lakkoofsa Afaan Oromoo

Afaan Oromoo keessatti lakkoofsi maqaa ofii qaba. Lakkoofsa 1 hanga 10 baruun jalqabna — kun bu'uura herrega hundaa dha.

📚 Lakkoofsa 1–10 / Numbers 1–10

1 = Tokko  |  2 = Lama  |  3 = Sadii  |  4 = Afur  |  5 = Shan
6 = Ja'a  |  7 = Torba  |  8 = Saddeet  |  9 = Sagal  |  10 = Kudhan

Ida'uu (Addition / +)

Ida'uun lakkoofsota lama walitti dabaluu dha. Afaan Oromoo keessatti "ida'uu" ykn "dabaluu" jedhama.

  • Tokko + Lama = Sadii → 1 + 2 = 3
  • Afur + Shan = Sagal → 4 + 5 = 9
  • Kudhan + Kudhan = Digdama → 10 + 10 = 20

Hir'isuu (Subtraction / −)

Hir'isuun lakkoofsa tokkoo irraa biraa kaasuu dha. "Hir'isuu" ykn "baasuu" jedhama.

  • Kudhan − Afur = Ja'a → 10 − 4 = 6
  • Sagal − Sadii = Ja'a → 9 − 3 = 6

Baay'isuu (Multiplication / ×)

Baay'isuun lakkoofsa yeroo murtaa'ame dabaluu dha. "Baay'isuu" jedhama.

  • Lama × Sadii = Ja'a → 2 × 3 = 6
  • Afur × Shan = Digdama → 4 × 5 = 20

Hiruu (Division / ÷)

Hiruun lakkoofsa qoqqooduu dha. "Hiruu" jedhama.

  • Kudhan ÷ Lama = Shan → 10 ÷ 2 = 5
  • Sagal ÷ Sadii = Sadii → 9 ÷ 3 = 3
💡 Lakkoofsa Guguddaa / Bigger Numbers

20 = Digdama  |  30 = Soddomaa  |  40 = Afurtamaa  |  50 = Shantamaa
100 = Dhibba  |  1000 = Kuma
