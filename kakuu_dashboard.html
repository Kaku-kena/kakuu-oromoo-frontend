Qubee Afaan Oromoo Maali?

Afaan Oromoon qubee Latin fayyadama — Qubee Oromoo jedhama. Bara 1991 irraa kaasee barreeffama official ta'e. Qubee 26 of keessaa qaba, kan Ingliffaa wajjin wal-fakkaatu, garuu sagalee adda addaa qaba.

Qubeelee Gurguddoo

Qubeelee Afaan Oromoo kanneen barnoota jalqabaatiif barbaachisan:

  • A a — sagalee "a" — fakkeenya: Aadaa (culture)
  • B b — sagalee "b" — fakkeenya: Barnoota (education)
  • D d — sagalee "d" — fakkeenya: Daa'ima (child)
  • E e — sagalee "e" — fakkeenya: Eegaa (wait)
  • F f — sagalee "f" — fakkeenya: Fayyaa (health)

Jechoota Furtuu

Jechoota Afaan Oromoo salphaa kanneen barbaachisaa ta'an:

  • Nagaa — Peace / Hello
  • Galatoomaa — Thank you
  • Maaloo — Please
  • Eeyyee — Yes
  • Lakki — No
